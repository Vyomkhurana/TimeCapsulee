How Time Capsule Work's

Preserve your memories and send messages to the future.

1️⃣ Register & Login

Create an account and log in to start using Time Capsule.

2️⃣ Create a Time Capsule

Write a message, upload media, and select a future date for delivery.

3️⃣ Secure Storage

Your time capsule is safely stored in our encrypted database.

4️⃣ Scheduled Email Delivery

On the selected date, your message will be sent to the recipient via email.

5️⃣ Relive Your Memories

Access and manage all your time capsules in your dashboard.
